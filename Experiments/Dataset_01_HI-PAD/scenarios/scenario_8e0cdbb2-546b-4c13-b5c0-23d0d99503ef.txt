Upon the discovery of a new intergalactic species visiting Earth, North Africa found itself in the midst of political unrest. Philosophical debates arose as to how to approach and interact with these beings, with some advocating for peaceful communication and others demanding defensive measures. The typical severity of political tensions in the region was heightened as governments struggled to come to a consensus on the matter. Meanwhile, the intergalactic visitors observed from afar, their own philosophies and beliefs regarding their place in the universe unknown to humanity.
