Heavily relying on her laptop to prepare for an important court case, a young lawyer from Europe was suddenly confronted with a disruptive technological failure. As she was about to finalize her arguments, her laptop suddenly shut down, leaving her helpless and worried about the consequences of not being able to submit her work on time. Despite her attempts to fix the issue, the device remained unresponsive, and she realized that she might have lost all her files and research material. Frustrated and anxious, she had to find a quick solution to recover her data and meet the deadline. The legal domain required her to comply with strict regulations, and any delay or mistake could have severe consequences for her client's case.
