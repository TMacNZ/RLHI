Totally unexpected violence erupted in a small, rural village in Subsaharan Africa. It all started online, when a group of students from a nearby school began sharing hateful messages about another group of students from a different school. The insults quickly escalated and turned into threats of physical harm. Despite efforts from school officials to intervene and resolve the conflict, tensions continued to rise. Eventually, a group of students from the first school showed up at the second school, armed with weapons and ready for a fight. The violence quickly spread throughout the village, leaving several people injured and traumatized. The incident highlighted the need for stronger measures to prevent online bullying and hate speech, as well as the importance of conflict resolution and peace education in schools.
