In a small village in rural Eurasia, a nuclear family is facing a life-threatening situation due to a cultural social challenge. The actual problem is that their daughter is in love with a man from a different caste, which is strictly forbidden in their community. The family is torn between their love for their daughter and their fear of the social consequences of breaking the cultural norms. The situation is complicated further by the fact that their daughter's lover is from a neighboring village, and the two villages have a long-standing rivalry. The family is struggling to find a way to keep their daughter safe while also honoring their cultural traditions, but time is running out. If they don't find a solution soon, their daughter's life could be in danger. It's a heart-wrenching situation that highlights the complex and sometimes dangerous consequences of cultural norms and social challenges in the region.
