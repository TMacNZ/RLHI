As a global scientific organization, it is critically important for us to manage the impact of political unrest in the Pacific region. The instability caused by protests and clashes between different factions has the potential to disrupt our research and development projects in the area. Our teams on the ground are working tirelessly to ensure the safety of our staff and assets, while also maintaining our commitment to advancing scientific knowledge.
