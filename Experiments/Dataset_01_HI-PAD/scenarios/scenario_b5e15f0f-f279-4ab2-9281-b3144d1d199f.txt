In the aftermath of a devastating natural disaster that had hit Europe, the emotional toll on survivors was palpable. Many found themselves grappling with feelings of grief, disillusionment, and frustration, as they struggled to come to terms with the extent of the destruction that had taken place. To make matters worse, resources were scarce, and tensions were running high as people vied for the limited aid that was available. In this environment, even minor annoyances could quickly escalate into heated interpersonal conflicts, as individuals jostled for position and resources. It was a difficult time, and many were left feeling overwhelmed and uncertain about the future.
