In Europe, a group of economic professionals found themselves in a life-threatening situation due to a mundane issue. The provision of a crucial resource was suddenly cut off, leaving them without the necessary supplies to complete their work. With no solution in sight, tensions began to rise as the professionals grew increasingly desperate. As time went on, the situation became more dire, and it became clear that without a resolution, their work and possibly even their lives were at risk. They scrambled to come up with alternative solutions, but each one fell short of what was needed. It seemed that there was no way out of this economic nightmare.
