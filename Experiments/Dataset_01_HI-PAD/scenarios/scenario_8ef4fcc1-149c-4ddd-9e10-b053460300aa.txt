John had been working so hard to save up for his new car, but he never expected that a simple flame would disrupt his plans. One night, as he was cooking his dinner, a small flame caught onto his curtains, and before he knew it, the whole room was on fire. John managed to escape unscathed, but his apartment was severely damaged. The fire department came in to put out the fire, but the damage was already done. John had to spend all the money he had saved up for his car on fixing his apartment, leaving him with nothing for his dream car. It was a mundane issue, but it completely disrupted his economic plans.
