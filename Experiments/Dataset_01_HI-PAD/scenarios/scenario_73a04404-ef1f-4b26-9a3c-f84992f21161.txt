In the far reaches of the cosmos, a dangerous argument had erupted among a group of celestial beings. They were debating over the access to a powerful universal energy source that was located in the South Asian region of the universe. The disagreement had become emotional, with each being convinced that they deserved exclusive control over the resource. As the argument escalated, the tension grew and threatened to cause a catastrophic explosion that could have devastating consequences for the entire universe. The stakes were high and the emotions were intense, making it difficult for the beings to find common ground and resolve the disagreement. It seemed that the fate of the cosmos hung in the balance, and a peaceful resolution seemed increasingly unlikely.
