The cream-colored walls of the conference room felt suffocating as the CEO of the company tried to explain the dire financial situation to his board members. The cream of the crop of the nation's top investors had gathered here, in the heart of Eurasia, to discuss the economic crisis that had hit the company hard. The stakes were high, and the tension in the room was palpable. They had to find a way out of this mess, or the company would go under. The cream of the staff had already been laid off, and more drastic measures were being considered. The CEO knew that he had to find a solution soon, or his reputation and the company's future would be forever tarnished.
