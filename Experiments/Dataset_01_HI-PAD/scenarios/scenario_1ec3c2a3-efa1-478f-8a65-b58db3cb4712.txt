In a small neighborhood of Eurasia, a financial crisis has erupted which has left the community in deep distress. The residents are struggling to make ends meet as their businesses and jobs have been impacted by the economic downturn. Amidst the chaos, philosophical debates have emerged as people question the morality of a system that allows for such extreme inequalities. Many are wondering if the pursuit of profit has become the sole purpose of existence, at the expense of human well-being. As the community comes together to support each other, they are also searching for answers to these deeper questions about the meaning of life and the role of money in society.
