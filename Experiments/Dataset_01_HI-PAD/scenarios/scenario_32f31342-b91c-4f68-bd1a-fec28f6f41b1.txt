In the midst of an intergalactic dispute, tensions rise between two Middle Eastern factions, resulting in a violent clash that leaves numerous individuals sick and injured. The conflict quickly spirals out of control as both sides refuse to back down, leading to a prolonged legal battle over who is responsible for the damages and who should be held accountable for the sick and injured. As the legal battle unfolds, the situation becomes increasingly complex, with each side presenting their own version of events and evidence to support their claims. Despite the severity of the situation, however, it remains an ordinary occurrence in the grand scheme of intergalactic conflicts and disputes.
