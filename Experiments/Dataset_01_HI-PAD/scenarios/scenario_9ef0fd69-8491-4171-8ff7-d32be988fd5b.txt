Sophie, a bright student in her final year of university, has always been a hard worker. She has never missed a deadline and always puts in extra effort to ensure her work is of the highest quality. However, as she approaches her final exams, she finds herself struggling to keep up with the workload. Desperate to succeed and maintain her perfect record, Sophie turns to her professor for help. Her professor offers her a solution: he can provide her with the exam questions in advance, giving her an unfair advantage over her classmates. Sophie is torn. On the one hand, she knows that cheating is wrong and goes against her ethical principles. On the other hand, she is terrified of failing and ruining her future. As the exam approaches, Sophie must make a difficult decision that could have life-threatening consequences for her academic career.
