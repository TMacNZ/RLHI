In the far reaches of the Intergalactic Federation, a disruptive movement has taken hold, threatening to rid the region of its intellectual elite. In the heart of Eurasia, a growing political unrest is targeting the most brilliant minds in the galaxy, with violent protests erupting in major cities and academic institutions. Intellectual leaders are being targeted and forced to flee for their safety, leaving a void in the region's scientific and academic communities. The ramifications of this movement could be catastrophic, as the loss of these brilliant minds could set back progress and innovation for generations to come. The Intergalactic Federation must act quickly to quell the unrest and protect its intellectual assets before it's too late.
