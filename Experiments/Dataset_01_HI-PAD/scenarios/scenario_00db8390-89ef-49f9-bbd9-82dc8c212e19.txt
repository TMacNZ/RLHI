The cosmic director gazed down at Earth, watching over the vast universe. As he looked closer to North America, he noticed a peculiar situation. A renowned director had just released a new film that was causing quite the stir in the industry. The problem was that the director had taken credit for the work of several of his assistants. The cosmic director pondered this ethical dilemma, knowing that it was an insignificant issue in the grand scheme of the universe. However, he couldn't shake the feeling that something needed to be done to rectify the situation. He knew that it was important to uphold integrity and honesty in all domains, including the physical world of filmmaking. The cosmic director decided to send a subtle message to the director, urging him to do the right thing and give credit where credit was due. The director eventually listened and publicly acknowledged the contributions of his assistants, setting a positive example for others in the industry to follow.
