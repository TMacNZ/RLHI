Politics has always been a universal topic that brings annoyance to many people around the world, but in South Asia, it has taken on a new dimension. With the region facing environmental issues that threaten the very existence of its people, politics has become a topic of intellectual debate. The need to address these issues has led to political polarization, with some leaders pushing for immediate action while others delay and deny the severity of the situation. The impact of climate change has been felt in South Asia through rising temperatures, unpredictable weather patterns, and the destruction of vital ecosystems. The politics of this issue has become a hot topic, with scientists, policymakers, and citizens alike debating the best way forward. As the region continues to grapple with environmental challenges, it is clear that politics will play a crucial role in shaping its future.
