The political unrest in Subsaharan Africa has left many individuals feeling trapped and helpless. The system they once relied on to protect them has failed, leaving them vulnerable to violence and instability. For those who have lost loved ones or been personally victimized by the chaos, the emotional toll is severe. Fear and anxiety have become constants in their daily lives, making it difficult to trust anyone or anything. Many are left wondering if there will ever be a solution to the turmoil that has upended their world. Despite their struggles, however, these individuals remain resilient, fighting to maintain a sense of hope and safety in a system that has let them down.
