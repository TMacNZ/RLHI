After months spent exploring the South Pacific with their chosen family, a group of friends find themselves facing an ethical dilemma. One member of the group has been consuming large amounts of alcohol and engaging in reckless behavior, putting themselves and others in danger. The group is torn between wanting to support their friend and feeling the need to intervene for everyone's safety. They try to approach the situation delicately, but tensions rise as the friend becomes defensive and refuses to acknowledge the impact of their actions. As the days pass, the group grapples with the complexity of the situation and the potential consequences of confronting their friend. Ultimately, they must decide whether to prioritize their loyalty and love for their chosen family member or prioritize the safety and well-being of the group as a whole.
