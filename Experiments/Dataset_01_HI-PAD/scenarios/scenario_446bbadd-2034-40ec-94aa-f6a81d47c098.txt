In the year 2275, the intergalactic economy was on the brink of collapse. The ratio of debt to GDP had reached unprecedented levels, and the vast majority of planets in the European region were on the brink of bankruptcy. The situation was dangerous, and many feared that it could lead to a catastrophic financial crisis that would spread throughout the galaxy. The economic domain was in turmoil, with businesses struggling to stay afloat and interplanetary trade grinding to a halt. Governments were scrambling to find a solution, but it seemed that there was no easy way out of this mess. As tensions rose and despair grew, there was a sense that the fate of the entire galaxy was at stake.
