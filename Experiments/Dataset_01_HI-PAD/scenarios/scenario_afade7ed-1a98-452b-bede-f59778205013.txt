In the Pacific region, an educational institution is facing a typical but mundane issue in their online platform - the risk of cyber attacks. With the increasing reliance on online resources for education, the institution is aware of the potential consequences of a security breach. They understand that it could lead to compromised student data and sensitive information being leaked. To address this risk, the institution has implemented strict security measures such as firewalls, regular software updates, and employee training on cyber safety. Despite these precautions, the risk of a cyber attack remains a constant concern for the institution. Therefore, they continue to monitor and assess their online security protocols to ensure the safety of their students and staff.
