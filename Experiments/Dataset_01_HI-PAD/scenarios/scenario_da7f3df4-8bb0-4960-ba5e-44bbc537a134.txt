It was supposed to be a joyous family gathering in Eurasia, with everyone excited to celebrate the matriarch's 80th birthday. But things took a turn for the worse when a mean-spirited argument erupted between two cousins over an old family feud. The argument quickly escalated into a physical altercation, with one of the cousins shoving the other down a flight of stairs. The victim suffered irreversible injuries, leaving them paralyzed from the waist down. The incident left the entire family reeling and struggling to come to terms with the severity of the situation. The once-close family now feels fractured and torn apart, with the memory of the incident haunting them for years to come.
