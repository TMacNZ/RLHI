In the extended family of Miguel, there was a rising conflict between two of his cousins, Juan and Carlos, who were both known to be agents of different political parties. Juan was a supporter of the newly elected president, while Carlos was a vocal critic. Their disagreements were usually heated but manageable until one fateful day when they got into a physical altercation during a family gathering. Carlos ended up with a broken leg, and Juan was charged with assault. The situation escalated quickly, and soon their respective political parties got involved, leading to irreparable damage not only to their relationship but to the entire family as well. It became a political issue in the region, and the family's reputation was tarnished. It was a sad turn of events that could not be undone, and the family was left to pick up the pieces of what was once a close-knit and peaceful community.
