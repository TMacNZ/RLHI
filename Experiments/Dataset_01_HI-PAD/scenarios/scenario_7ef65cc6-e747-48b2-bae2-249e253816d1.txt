In the remote South Pacific, tensions have risen to dangerous levels as a result of political unrest. The situation has escalated to the point where legal action is imminent. The chosen family, a group of individuals who have come together to form a close-knit community, find themselves caught in the middle of the conflict. They had been living peacefully for years, relying on each other for support and protection. However, the political turmoil threatens to upend their perfectly crafted existence. The group must navigate this treacherous landscape, unsure of who they can trust and how to keep themselves safe. As tensions continue to rise, the chosen family must find a way to stick together and protect each other from the chaos that threatens to consume them all.
