In the vast Pacific region, a life-threatening accident has occurred in the middle of the desert. A group of hikers, who went on a day-long trek, lost their way and found themselves stranded in the scorching heat. With no food and water left, they are struggling to survive. The national authorities have been alerted and are frantically trying to locate the hikers. The situation is critical as the desert's extreme conditions can cause serious physical harm, and the hikers' lives are at risk. A rescue team has been dispatched, but the vastness of the desert makes it difficult to pinpoint their exact location. The clock is ticking, and every passing moment is a matter of life and death.
