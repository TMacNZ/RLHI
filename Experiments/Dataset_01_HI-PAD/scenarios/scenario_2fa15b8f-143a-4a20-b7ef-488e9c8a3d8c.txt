It was a typical evening in the bustling city of Dubai, where communities were tightly knit, and chosen families were of utmost importance. However, a catastrophic event was likely to shake the foundations of one such close-knit family. As they sat down for dinner, they received a call informing them that their beloved family member, who was on a business trip to Iraq, had been caught in a bomb blast. The news hit them like a ton of bricks, and they were left in a state of shock and despair. The mundane issue of missing someone suddenly became a matter of life and death, and the entire family was left grappling with the uncertainty of the situation. As they tried to come to terms with what had happened, they clung to each other for support, realizing that in times like these, their chosen family was all they had.
