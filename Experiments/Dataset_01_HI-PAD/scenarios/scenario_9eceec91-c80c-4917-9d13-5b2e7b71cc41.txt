Mrs. Gupta, a matriarch of an extended family in South Asia, is deeply concerned about the environmental issues plaguing her community. With her intellectual prowess and passion for sustainable living, Mrs. Gupta has taken it upon herself to educate her family members about the importance of reducing their carbon footprint. She has organized numerous workshops and seminars to raise awareness about the harmful effects of pollution, deforestation, and climate change. Despite the ordinary severity of the situation, Mrs. Gupta remains determined to make a positive impact and inspire her family to adopt eco-friendly practices. She believes that small changes in daily habits, such as using reusable bags and reducing water usage, can make a significant difference in preserving the environment for future generations.
