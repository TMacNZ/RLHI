In the arid deserts of North Africa, a young couple finds themselves in the midst of a financial crisis. They are deeply in love and have always dreamt of a grand wedding celebration, but their current circumstances don't allow for such extravagance. They are struggling to make ends meet in a region where resources are scarce, and the unforgiving climate makes it even harder to survive. The couple is torn between their desire for a beautiful wedding and the reality of their financial situation. They know that they must prioritize their environmental needs over their personal desires if they want to have a sustainable future together. They come to the conclusion that they will have a simple and intimate ceremony that doesn't cost them a lot of money. They will use the money they saved to invest in their future and the environment around them. The couple realizes that their love is worth more than any grand celebration and that they can build a beautiful life together with hard work and dedication.
