In the aftermath of a severe natural disaster that struck South Asia, online platforms were being used to perform rescue operations and provide aid to those affected. Social media was flooded with messages and posts from people who were stranded and seeking help, while others were offering food, shelter, and medical assistance to those in need. Relief organizations were also using online platforms to coordinate their efforts and ensure that aid was reaching the affected areas. Despite the challenges posed by disrupted communications and power outages, the people of South Asia came together to perform acts of compassion and generosity, demonstrating the power of social media to unite communities in times of crisis.
