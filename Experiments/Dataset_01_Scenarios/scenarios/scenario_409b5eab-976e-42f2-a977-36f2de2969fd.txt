Juan had been waiting for the signal to start his small business for months. He had saved up enough money and had a solid business plan, but the economy in South America was unpredictable. He was hesitant to invest his life savings without a clear sign of stability. Unfortunately, the signal never came. The economic conditions remained turbulent, and Juan could not take the risk of losing his hard-earned money. He had to put his dreams on hold and find a stable job to support his family. The situation was frustrating and disheartening, but Juan knew he had to prioritize his financial security. He hoped for a better future and continued to keep an eye out for a positive signal in the economy.
