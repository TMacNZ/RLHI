In the Middle East, the wealthy were accustomed to conducting all their financial transactions online. Their vast fortunes were stored in digital wallets, accessible through a variety of apps and platforms. However, a catastrophic event occurred when a technological glitch wiped out all the digital wallets, leaving the wealthy unable to access their money. Panic spread quickly as people realized they had no way to pay for basic necessities, let alone maintain their lavish lifestyles. The financial crisis that ensued was unprecedented, with businesses shutting down and people losing their homes. The Middle East was plunged into chaos as the wealthy struggled to find a solution to this technological nightmare.
