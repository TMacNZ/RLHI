In North America, the issue of safety has become a catastrophic health concern. The cultural domain has played a significant role in exacerbating the problem. Due to cultural practices and beliefs, many people are not taking the necessary precautions to ensure their safety. This has led to an alarming increase in accidents and fatalities across the region. Despite numerous public awareness campaigns, people are still not taking safety seriously. The severity of the problem has reached a critical point and urgent action is needed to address it. If nothing is done, the consequences could be devastating not only for individuals but for society as a whole.
