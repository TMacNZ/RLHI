The Johnson family had always been a close-knit nuclear family living in the suburbs of a bustling city. However, they soon realized that their peaceful existence was about to be disrupted by the political unrest that was brewing in the country. The economic domain was particularly affected, and the Johnsons found themselves struggling to make ends meet as prices of essential commodities soared. With mounting pressure to maintain their standard of living, the family was constantly on edge, and even small disagreements would quickly escalate into full-blown arguments. As the political situation worsened, they knew that they had to stick together and support each other through these challenging times.
