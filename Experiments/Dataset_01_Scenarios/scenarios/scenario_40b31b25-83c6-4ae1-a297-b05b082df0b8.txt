In the heart of North America, a chosen family finds themselves in a life-threatening situation due to an intense disagreement with their once close friend. What started as a minor argument over a difference in opinion has now escalated to a full-blown emotional battle. The enemy, who was once considered a member of the chosen family, has become a looming threat, stirring up chaos and tension in the household. The family is now forced to confront their emotional scars and past traumas as they fight to protect their safety and unity. As the situation intensifies, they are left wondering if they will ever be able to reconcile with their former friend or if they will have to sever ties permanently to protect themselves from harm.
