The people of Subsaharan Africa are facing a long-lasting health concern due to the environmental domain. The air and water pollution are causing severe respiratory diseases, skin infections, and other life-threatening illnesses. The severity of this situation is alarming as it's affecting people of all age groups. The international community needs to take immediate action to address this issue. The locals are shouting for help, but their voices are not being heard. It's high time to take this matter seriously and take necessary steps to prevent this environmental disaster from becoming a catastrophe.
