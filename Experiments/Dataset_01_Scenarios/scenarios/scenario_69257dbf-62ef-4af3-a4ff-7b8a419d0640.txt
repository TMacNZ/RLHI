In the bustling city of Mumbai, India, political unrest has reached catastrophic levels due to the lack of consideration for environmental factors. Pollution levels have skyrocketed, with the air quality index reaching dangerous levels, causing respiratory problems for the citizens. The water bodies in and around the city have been contaminated, leading to the loss of aquatic life and posing a threat to the health of the people who rely on it for their daily needs. The government's failure to address these issues has led to protests and riots, resulting in a state of unrest throughout the city.
