In North Africa, a political crisis erupted when a group of citizens suffered life-threatening injuries due to a mysterious skin condition. Panic and fear spread throughout the nation as more and more people began to exhibit the same symptoms. The government was quick to respond, but their initial efforts were met with skepticism and criticism from the public. As the situation worsened, tensions between the government and the people reached a boiling point. Riots and protests broke out, and accusations of negligence and corruption flew. The nation was on the brink of collapse, and the fate of its people hung in the balance.
