Despite the insignificant severity of the issue, it's an emotional topic for many who fear the impact of human activity on the local wildlife. One of the key concerns for these activists is the impact of online activities on the well-being of the local environment. As the city continues to grow and modernize, the use of digital technology is becoming increasingly prevalent. While this has brought many benefits, there are fears that it is also contributing to pollution and other environmental issues.
