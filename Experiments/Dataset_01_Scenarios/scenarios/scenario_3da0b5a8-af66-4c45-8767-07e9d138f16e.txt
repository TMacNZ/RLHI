The recent drought has determined the fate of many small communities in Subsaharan Africa, causing an annoyance to the local economies. This natural disaster has hit the region hard, leaving many farmers without crops and livestock. Without the ability to produce goods, the economy has taken a major hit. The lack of resources has caused many businesses to close and unemployment to skyrocket. The local government is struggling to find a solution to this economic crisis and provide aid to those in need. The severity of this drought has emphasized the need for long-term solutions to ensure the survival of these communities in the future.
