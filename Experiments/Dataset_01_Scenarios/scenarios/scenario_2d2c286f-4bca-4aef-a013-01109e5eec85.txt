The Smith family had always been a close-knit nuclear family, but their world was turned upside down when the father was diagnosed with a terminal illness. The news of his impending death was devastating for the family, both emotionally and economically. The father had been the sole breadwinner of the family, and without his income, they were facing a financial crisis. The cost of his treatment was exorbitant, and they had to dip into their savings to afford it. With the father's imminent passing, the family was left with a looming uncertainty about their future. They would have to adjust to a new normal, one that involved a significant loss and a drastic change in their way of life.
