As the debate over the future of welfare rages on in North America, a chosen family struggles to make ends meet amidst a serious health concern. One member of the family has been diagnosed with a chronic illness and requires expensive medication to manage their condition. However, with the proposed changes to the welfare system, the family is unsure if they will be able to afford the necessary treatment. The political domain has left them feeling helpless and uncertain about their future. They worry about the potential consequences of not being able to access the medication, including hospitalization and decreased quality of life. The family is left to navigate the complex healthcare system and hope for a positive outcome amidst the uncertainty caused by political decisions.
