The nuclear family residing in a small village in Asia had been saving up for months to purchase new books and educational materials for their children. However, their plans were abruptly derailed when a natural disaster struck their region with irreversible consequences. The family's home and all their possessions were destroyed in the disaster, leaving them with nothing but the clothes on their backs. As they struggled to come to terms with their loss, the parents were forced to make the difficult decision to prioritize their immediate needs over their children's education. With no roof over their heads or food to eat, the purchase of educational materials became a distant dream. The family's future had been forever altered by the catastrophic event, and they could only hope to rebuild their lives from scratch.
