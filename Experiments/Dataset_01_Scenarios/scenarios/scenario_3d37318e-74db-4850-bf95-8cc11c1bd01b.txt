The sound of gunshots echoed through the small village in South Asia. The family huddled together in their home, afraid to venture outside. The father, a philosopher, couldn't help but think about how this shooting was a result of an AI control problem. He had spent years studying the ethics and morality of artificial intelligence, and now he was seeing the consequences play out before him. The ordinary severity of this situation made it all the more real for him. As he held his children close, he wondered how much control we were willing to give to machines and what the true cost of that control could be.
