In a small neighborhood in Eurasia, tensions were rising as a group of locals were protesting the employment of outsiders at a local business. The community had always prided themselves on their cultural identity and felt that employing outsiders was disrupting their way of life. The protest began peacefully, but quickly escalated into violence as the group became more agitated. The police were called in to intervene, but struggled to control the situation as the crowd grew larger and more aggressive. The conflict between those in favor of employing outsiders and those against it continued to intensify, causing a deep divide within the community. As the situation grew more volatile, it became clear that a resolution would not be easily reached, leaving the future of the community uncertain.
