In South Asia, political unrest had been brewing for months, and it had reached a boiling point. The extended family of a wealthy businessman was in grave danger as their opulent mansion became a target for the angry mob. The family had been enjoying their lavish lifestyle while the majority of the population struggled to make ends meet. The mob had grown tired of this inequality and decided to take matters into their own hands. The family barricaded themselves inside the mansion, but the mob was relentless in their pursuit. The family feared for their lives as they heard the mob chanting outside, demanding justice and an end to their wealth. As the night wore on, the family realized that they had to make a desperate decision to escape with their lives. They managed to slip out of the mansion through a secret passage and fled in the cover of darkness. The family's wealth had become a liability, and they were forced to leave everything behind in order to survive.
