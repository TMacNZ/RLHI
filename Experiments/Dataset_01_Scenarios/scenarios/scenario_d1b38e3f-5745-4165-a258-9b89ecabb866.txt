In a distant galaxy far from Earth, a dangerous problem has arisen. The consumption rate of a vital resource required for intergalactic travel has skyrocketed to unsustainable levels. The entire Eurasian region is feeling the impact of this mundane issue, as the physical supply of the resource dwindles to dangerously low levels. Without this resource, entire civilizations will be cut off from the rest of the galaxy, unable to explore, trade or communicate. The situation is dire, and the leaders of the affected planets must come together to find a solution before it's too late. The fate of their intergalactic society rests in their hands.
