However, they had a disagreement about the correct way to complete a task that was life-threatening. Their argument quickly escalated into a heated argument and both guys were unwilling to back down. Their cultural differences made it difficult for them to see eye to eye, and they both held firmly to their own beliefs. This disagreement had the potential to cause serious harm if not resolved. The team leader had to intervene and mediate the situation before it got out of hand. It was a tense moment, but eventually, the two men were able to put their differences aside and agree on the best way to proceed with the task at hand.
