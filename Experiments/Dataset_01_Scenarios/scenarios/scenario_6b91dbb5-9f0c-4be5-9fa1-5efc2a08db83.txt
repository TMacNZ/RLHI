In the year 2300, an intergalactic ghost has been haunting the skies of Asia. The ghostly apparition, which appears to be a large amorphous cloud of ethereal energy, has been causing severe environmental issues across the region. The ghost's presence has been linked to a significant increase in air pollution and radiation levels, which have been causing health problems for the local population. The legal ramifications of this situation have been vast, with various organizations and governments vying for control over the investigation and potential solution to the problem. Meanwhile, the ghost continues to loom ominously over the region, its spectral presence a constant reminder of the environmental damage it is causing.
