In a distant galaxy far from Earth, a search for resources had led to the irreversible destruction of a planet in the South Asian region. The physical devastation caused by the mining operations had triggered a wave of political unrest among the inhabitants of neighboring planets. The intergalactic community was in chaos with protests and riots spreading like wildfire. The situation had escalated to a point where peace negotiations seemed impossible, and the possibility of a full-scale war was looming. The leaders of the affected planets were in a deadlock, and the fate of the entire region was at stake. The search for resources had turned into a catastrophic event, and the consequences would be felt for generations to come.
