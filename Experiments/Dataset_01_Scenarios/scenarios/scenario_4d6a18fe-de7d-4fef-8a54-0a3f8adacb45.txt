In a small, tight-knit neighborhood in Subsaharan Africa, the financial crisis had hit hard. Families were struggling to make ends meet, and many were unable to put food on the table. The local community rallied together to try and help, but tensions were high as resources were scarce. Emotions ran high as parents worried about how they would feed their children and put a roof over their heads. The stress of the situation caused arguments and fights to break out among neighbors who were usually friendly with one another. Despite their best efforts, the community was still struggling to cope with the financial crisis, and the emotional toll it was taking on everyone was becoming more and more apparent.
