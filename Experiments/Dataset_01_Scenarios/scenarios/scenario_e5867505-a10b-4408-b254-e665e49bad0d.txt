As the financial crisis continues to grip Europe, many individuals are finding themselves in dangerous situations. One such person is a young woman who has become obsessed with a mysterious object she found on the side of the road. She can't explain why, but she feels that the object holds the key to her financial future. She spends all of her spare time researching it, neglecting her job and social life in the process. As her obsession grows, she begins to take dangerous risks in order to acquire more information about the object. She maxes out her credit cards, takes out loans, and even starts selling her possessions to fund her obsession. Her friends and family try to intervene, but she is too far gone. What she doesn't realize is that the object is just a worthless trinket, and her obsession with it has put her in serious financial jeopardy.
