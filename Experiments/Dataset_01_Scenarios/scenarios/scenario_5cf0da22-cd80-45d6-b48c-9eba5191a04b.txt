In the Middle East, achieving global educational standards has become an irreversible issue that affects millions of students. While progress has been made in recent years, there are still mundane issues that hinder students from achieving their full potential. Lack of access to quality education, limited resources, and outdated teaching methods are just a few of the challenges facing students and educators. In order to address these issues, innovative solutions are needed to transform the educational domain in the region. It is imperative that all stakeholders work together to support the next generation of leaders and ensure that they have the skills and knowledge necessary to compete on a global scale.
