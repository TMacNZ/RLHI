In a small village in rural Asia, a group of friends gathered to play their favorite sport - soccer. Excitement was in the air as they began to kick the ball around. Suddenly, one of the players made a hard tackle on another, causing him to fall awkwardly and hit his head on a rock. The severity of the incident became immediately apparent as the injured player lost consciousness and started convulsing. The group was in a state of panic as they realized the irreversible damage that had been done. They rushed their friend to the hospital, but it was too late. The player suffered from a severe intellectual disability as a result of the accident, leaving him unable to continue playing the sport he loved. The group of friends was left with a heavy burden, as they were responsible for the accident and the devastating consequences that followed.
