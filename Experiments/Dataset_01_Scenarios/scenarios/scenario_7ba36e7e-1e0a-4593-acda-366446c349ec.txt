In the vast expanse of the universe, a group of wealthy North American entrepreneurs have discovered a new technology that could change the course of history. This technology has the ability to manipulate time and space, allowing them to travel through wormholes and explore the far corners of the universe. Their newfound power has caused a great ethical dilemma, as they debate whether they should share this technology with the rest of the world or keep it for themselves and their rich friends. The potential disruption to the natural order of the universe is immense, and the consequences of their decision could be catastrophic. As they weigh the pros and cons of their options, the fate of the universe hangs in the balance. Will they choose to share their wealth and knowledge, or keep it for their own selfish gain? The answer remains to be seen.
