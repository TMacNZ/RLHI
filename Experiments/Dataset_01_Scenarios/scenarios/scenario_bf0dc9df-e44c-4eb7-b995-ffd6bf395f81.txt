It was a lovely day in the Middle East, with the sun shining bright in the sky. But the mood was far from lovely as the financial crisis had hit the region hard. People were struggling to make ends meet, and the emotional toll of the situation was palpable. Families were worried about putting food on the table and paying for basic necessities. The crisis had affected not only individuals but also businesses, causing widespread layoffs and bankruptcies. With the situation showing no signs of improvement, the emotional stress and anxiety were only getting worse. The typical severity of the crisis had turned it into a continental problem, with people across the region feeling the impact. It was a difficult time for everyone, and the hope for a better tomorrow was the only thing keeping them going.
