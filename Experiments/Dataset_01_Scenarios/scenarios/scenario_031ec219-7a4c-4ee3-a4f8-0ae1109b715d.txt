In the year 2150, a dangerous technological failure occurred in North America that had severe political consequences. The interstellar portrait capturing the leaders of North America malfunctioned, causing it to emit a powerful radiation that affected the entire region. The leaders who were present at the time of the incident were disintegrated on the spot, leaving the country without any direction. Chaos ensued as the citizens demanded answers and the neighboring countries grew increasingly concerned about the situation. It was later discovered that the portrait's technology had been tampered with by a group of political dissidents who wanted to overthrow the government. The incident sparked a political crisis that lasted for years and left a lasting impact on the region's political landscape.
