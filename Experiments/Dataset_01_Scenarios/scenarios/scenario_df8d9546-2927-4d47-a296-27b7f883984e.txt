As tensions rise within the government, a national decision must be made regarding the irreversible damage being done to the environment in the Pacific region. Environmental activists have been asking for stricter regulations on companies and industries that are contributing to pollution and destruction of the ecosystem. However, political unrest has made it difficult for any action to be taken. The fate of the Pacific region's environment hangs in the balance as officials must ask themselves if they are willing to prioritize the future of the planet over their own political agendas.
