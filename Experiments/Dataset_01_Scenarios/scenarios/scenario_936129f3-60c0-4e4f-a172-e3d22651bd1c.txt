Despite the insignificant severity of the situation, the European legal system is facing a challenge in identifying the source of online political unrest. With the rise of social media and the internet, it has become increasingly difficult to track down the origin of controversial content without violating privacy laws. The challenge lies in balancing the need to uphold freedom of speech with the responsibility to prevent hate speech and incitement to violence. The legal system is constantly adapting and implementing new strategies to tackle this issue, but it remains a complex and ongoing battle.
