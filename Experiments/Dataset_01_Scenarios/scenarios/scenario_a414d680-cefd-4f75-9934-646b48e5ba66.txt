In the midst of intergalactic tension, a handful of political leaders in Eurasia engage in a volatile power struggle that threatens the lives of millions. With the fate of the region hanging in the balance, a delicate balance of alliances and treaties is at risk of collapsing under the weight of greed and ambition. As tensions escalate and violence erupts in the streets, ordinary citizens are caught in the crossfire, forced to choose sides and fight for their survival. The fate of the entire region rests on the actions of a few key players, each driven by their own motives and willing to do whatever it takes to come out on top. In this high-stakes game of politics and power, the cost of failure is measured in lives lost and a future forever changed.
