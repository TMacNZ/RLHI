Once the root cause of the malfunction has been identified, steps should be taken to repair or replace the faulty machinery. This could involve either repairing the machinery on-site or transporting it to a facility where the necessary repairs or replacements can be made.

In the long term, it is important to implement measures to prevent similar incidents from occurring in the future.
